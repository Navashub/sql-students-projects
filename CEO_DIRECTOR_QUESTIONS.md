# CEO/Director Review: Retail Sales Analytics Project
## Executive-Level Questions & Expected Answers

---

## 1. REVENUE & PROFITABILITY

### Question 1.1: "What is our total revenue and which product categories are driving the most value?"
**Expected Answer Should Include:**
- Total revenue across all transactions (1000 records)
- Revenue breakdown by product category (Beauty, Clothing, Electronics)
- Ranking of categories by revenue contribution
- Percentage of revenue from each category
- Insights on which category is the cash cow vs. emerging opportunities

**Red Flag if Missing:** No specific numbers, vague language about "strong" or "weak" categories without data

---

### Question 1.2: "Which quarter performed best and why? Are there seasonal trends we should plan for?"
**Expected Answer Should Include:**
- Revenue/transaction count by quarter (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)
- Identification of peak and low seasons
- Comparison of performance across quarters
- Potential reasons (e.g., holiday shopping, seasonal demand)
- Recommendation for inventory/marketing planning

**Red Flag if Missing:** No quarter-by-quarter breakdown, inability to explain seasonal patterns

---

### Question 1.3: "What is our average transaction value and how does it vary by product category?"
**Expected Answer Should Include:**
- Average transaction size overall
- Average by category (e.g., Electronics likely higher than Beauty/Clothing)
- Distribution of transaction sizes (small vs. large orders)
- Opportunity to upsell or increase basket size
- Category-specific pricing strategy implications

**Red Flag if Missing:** Only mentioning one or two categories, no comparison

---

## 2. CUSTOMER INSIGHTS

### Question 2.1: "Who are our most valuable customers? Can you segment them by demographics and purchasing behavior?"
**Expected Answer Should Include:**
- Customer segmentation by age groups (18-30, 31-45, 46-60, 60+)
- Gender distribution and purchasing patterns by gender
- High-value vs. low-value customer segments
- Repeat purchase behavior (if identifiable from customer_id)
- Recommendations for targeted marketing

**Red Flag if Missing:** No demographic analysis, treating all customers as one group

---

### Question 2.2: "What is the gender breakdown and do males and females prefer different categories?"
**Expected Answer Should Include:**
- Male vs. Female customer count
- Category preferences by gender (e.g., do women buy more Beauty products?)
- Revenue contribution by gender
- Any significant differences in average transaction value
- Implications for marketing and product placement

**Red Flag if Missing:** No gender analysis, or only mentioning it without specific insights

---

### Question 2.3: "Are we serving younger or older customers? Should we adjust our strategy for different age groups?"
**Expected Answer Should Include:**
- Distribution of customers across age groups
- Which age groups spend the most (by total and average)
- Category preferences by age (e.g., do older customers prefer certain products?)
- Lifetime value implications for different age segments
- Recommendation for market positioning

**Red Flag if Missing:** Treating all ages the same, no age-based segmentation

---

## 3. OPERATIONAL EFFICIENCY

### Question 3.1: "What is our average order size (quantity) and is there room to optimize pricing or bundling?"
**Expected Answer Should Include:**
- Average quantity per transaction
- Distribution of order sizes (1 unit vs. bulk)
- Price per unit analysis by category
- Opportunity for bundle deals or volume discounts
- Impact on gross margin if quantities change

**Red Flag if Missing:** No analysis of quantity trends, only focusing on revenue

---

### Question 3.2: "Do weekends perform differently than weekdays? Should we adjust staffing or promotions?"
**Expected Answer Should Include:**
- Weekend vs. weekday transaction count
- Revenue comparison between weekends and weekdays
- Category performance differences by day type
- Customer behavior patterns
- Staffing and inventory recommendations

**Red Flag if Missing:** No weekend analysis, missing this business opportunity

---

### Question 3.3: "Are there any slow-moving products or dead inventory we should address?"
**Expected Answer Should Include:**
- Products/categories with lowest sales volume or revenue
- Price points that aren't selling well
- Recommendation to discount, bundle, or discontinue
- Opportunity cost of shelf space

**Red Flag if Missing:** Only discussing winners, not addressing underperformers

---

## 4. GROWTH & STRATEGY

### Question 4.1: "What is our year-over-year growth? Are we on track to meet our targets?"
**Expected Answer Should Include:**
- Total revenue for the year 2023
- Comparison to prior year (if available in data)
- Growth rate or trend analysis
- Forecasting for next year based on current trends
- What would need to change to accelerate growth

**Red Flag if Missing:** No growth perspective, no forward-looking analysis

---

### Question 4.2: "Which category should we invest in or expand? Which should we consider de-emphasizing?"
**Expected Answer Should Include:**
- Growth rate of each category
- Profitability or margin analysis (if available)
- Customer preference trends
- Competitive positioning
- Strategic recommendation with supporting data

**Red Flag if Missing:** Vague recommendations without data backing

---

### Question 4.3: "What is our market opportunity? Are we underserving any segment?"
**Expected Answer Should Include:**
- Customer concentration (repeat vs. new customers)
- Geographic or demographic gaps
- Unmet needs in the data
- Addressable market expansion opportunities
- Recommendation for new customer acquisition

**Red Flag if Missing:** Only looking at current state, not future potential

---

## 5. DATA QUALITY & METHODOLOGY

### Question 5.1: "How reliable is this data? What assumptions did you make?"
**Expected Answer Should Include:**
- Data source and time period covered
- Any data quality issues or gaps
- Assumptions made in analysis (e.g., no duplicates, no missing values)
- Methodology for segmentation and calculations
- Confidence level in findings

**Red Flag if Missing:** Unaware of data limitations, no discussion of methodology

---

### Question 5.2: "Are there any outliers or unusual patterns we should investigate?"
**Expected Answer Should Include:**
- Any unusual transaction sizes or customer behavior
- Anomalies in the data
- Potential explanations or concerns
- Impact on the overall analysis

**Red Flag if Missing:** No data quality checks, accepting all data at face value

---

## 6. ACTIONABLE RECOMMENDATIONS

### Question 6.1: "Based on this analysis, what are your top 3 recommendations for the business?"
**Expected Answer Should Include:**
- Specific, data-backed recommendations
- Expected impact (revenue increase, cost savings, customer satisfaction)
- Implementation timeline and resource requirements
- Risk considerations
- Success metrics to track

**Red Flag if Missing:** Vague recommendations, no prioritization, no action plan

---

### Question 6.2: "What additional data or analysis would you need to support decision-making?"
**Expected Answer Should Include:**
- Gaps in current analysis
- Additional metrics to track (e.g., customer acquisition cost, lifetime value)
- Competitive benchmarking data
- Customer satisfaction/retention metrics
- Real-time vs. historical analysis needs

**Red Flag if Missing:** Thinking analysis is complete without identifying further needs

## 🎯 EVALUATION RUBRIC

| Criterion | Excellent | Good | Needs Improvement |
|-----------|-----------|------|-------------------|
| **Data Analysis** | Multiple dimensions analyzed (category, customer, time) | 2-3 dimensions analyzed | Only basic totals |
| **Insights** | Clear, actionable, data-backed | Some insights provided | Surface-level observations |
| **Business Acumen** | Strategic thinking, growth focus | Tactical observations | No business context |
| **Visualization** | Clear charts/dashboards | Some visuals | Text-only presentation |
| **Recommendations** | Specific, prioritized, measurable | General suggestions | Vague or missing |
| **Communication** | Clear narrative, professional | Organized but technical | Unclear or disorganized |
